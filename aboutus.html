Who We Are

Originally established in October 2010, the organization was formally registered in November 2016, Hexastra Infotech is a trusted Information Technology solutions provider based in Vasai, Mumbai, Maharashtra. We specialize in offering CCTV surveillance systems, computers, laptops, printers, and security access control solutions.

Over the years, we’ve built a reputation for delivering reliable, innovative, and cost-effective IT solutions that help clients focus on what matters most — growing their business.
